All About SIA Digital

SIA digital solutions was established in 2023 Singapore complementing the government's incentives in subsiding digitalisation, encouraging local businesses to have websites and to use online sales platforms. Starting from a team solely based in Singapore, now SIA digital solutions has expanded with a diverse team present in over 6 countries.

We aim to provide a tailor made solution to create a digital identity for any company, which improves a company’s recognition and sales, contributing to economic growth. With our headquarters in Singapore and Bangkok we serve a world class service to all businesses or organizations to achieve their goals, providing the best ROI with our services.

As a subsidiary of TEI Holdings we follow the same agendas and work ethics improving the business community providing life changing products to our customers.

Our Vision And Mission

Vision
